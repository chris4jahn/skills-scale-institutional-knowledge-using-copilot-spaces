# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers research user needs, design intuitive experiences, and validate design decisions through testing. They collaborate with Product and Engineering to ensure features are usable and meet user expectations.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, mockups, and prototypes
- Define user flows and interaction patterns
- Collaborate with PM on feature requirements and feasibility
- Maintain design systems and accessibility standards

### Goals
- Create intuitive, accessible user experiences
- Reduce user friction and support costs
- Ensure design consistency across the product

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and feedback sessions
- User research findings and insights
- Collaboration with PM and Developers on implementation feasibility

---

## Technical Writer

### Role Summary
Technical Writers create and maintain user-facing and internal documentation. They ensure documentation is accurate, up-to-date, and accessible to the intended audience.

### Responsibilities
- Write and update user guides, API documentation, and release notes
- Collaborate with Developers and PM to understand features
- Review documentation during planning and before releases
- Maintain documentation templates and style guides
- Ensure documentation is published and discoverable

### Goals
- Provide clear, accurate documentation for all features
- Reduce support burden through self-service resources
- Keep documentation synchronized with product releases

### Typical Communication
- Documentation reviews with Developers and PM
- Release notes coordination with Release Manager
- Feedback from Customer Support on common user questions

---

## Release Manager

### Role Summary
Release Managers coordinate and execute software releases. They ensure releases meet quality standards, follow the release process, and minimize disruption to users and operations.

### Responsibilities
- Plan and schedule releases with PM and Engineering
- Execute deployment checklists and verify release readiness
- Coordinate release communications to stakeholders and support teams
- Monitor deployments and coordinate rollback if needed
- Maintain release artifacts and version history

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, on-schedule releases
- Minimize production incidents and downtime
- Ensure smooth handoffs to support and operations

### Typical Communication
- Release planning meetings with PM and Engineering leads
- Go/no-go decisions before production deployments
- Release announcements and post-deployment summaries

---

## Customer Support Lead

### Role Summary
Customer Support Leads manage the support team and ensure customers receive timely, effective assistance. They gather feedback on product issues and feature requests to inform the product roadmap.

### Responsibilities
- Triage and prioritize customer issues
- Coordinate with Engineering on bug reports and escalations
- Provide product feedback based on support trends
- Prepare support team for upcoming releases with training materials
- Track support metrics and customer satisfaction

### Goals
- Resolve customer issues quickly and effectively
- Reduce support volume through improved documentation and product quality
- Represent customer voice in product decisions

### Typical Communication
- Weekly bug triage with Engineering and PM
- Release readiness briefings with Release Manager and Technical Writer
- Escalation of critical customer issues to PM and Engineering

---

## Security Champion

### Role Summary
Security Champions embed security practices into the development lifecycle. They advocate for secure design, review code for vulnerabilities, and respond to security incidents.

### Responsibilities
- Review designs and code for security vulnerabilities
- Ensure security scanning tools are integrated into CI/CD
- Coordinate security incident response and remediation
- Provide security training and guidance to the team
- Track and remediate security findings from scans and audits

### Goals
- Prevent security vulnerabilities from reaching production
- Ensure compliance with security policies and standards
- Foster a security-aware development culture

### Typical Communication
- Security reviews during design and code review
- Incident response coordination during security events
- Security status updates in planning and retrospectives

---

## How to use these persona definitions

### In Project Artifacts
- Reference personas in project plans and one-pagers to clarify responsibilities
- Assign personas to specific tasks in the backlog (e.g., "UX Designer: create mockups")
- Use personas in Definition of Done criteria (e.g., "Technical Writer: release notes published")

### In Meetings and Workflows
- Include relevant personas in kickoff meetings and planning sessions
- Assign personas to checklist items in release and execution processes
- Reference personas when escalating issues or requesting reviews

### With Copilot Spaces
- Use these persona definitions to frame scenarios and sample interactions in the Skills Exercise
- Each persona can be used as a persona prompt for Copilot Spaces to shape role-specific guidance
